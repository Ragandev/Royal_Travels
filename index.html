KODAIKANAL

3 Days / 5 Nights

Many of us have visited Kodaikanal whenever we had a chance, especially in the summer season. It is one of the well-known tourist spots in South India, and it's a dream destination for a rejuvenating retreat at the weekends. One thing you cannot miss while visiting Kodaikanal is the lake that is named after the hill station. Enjoying boat rides in the lake surrounded by mist will feel like something straight out of your dreams. The forests of Kodaikanal are a sight for sore eyes, and even the town of Kodaikanal is a beautiful resort for leisure walks. So, choose our travel services to let us take you on a perfect trip to the wonderful "Princess of Hill Station."

Mudumalai

3 Days / 5 Nights

Munnar is a magnificent hill station that resides in the mountain range called the Western Ghats. Even though it is famous for the visits of newly married couples, its heavenly outlook can win all our hearts with just a mere glance. Also, if you are lucky enough to visit Munnar during the flowering season of the Kurinji flowers, be prepared to be swept over by their breathtaking view. Munnar has rich wildlife, and it holds many surprises within itself for you to experience, like Virgin forests and massive waterfalls. If you haven't been to Munnar, be quick to book a trip to this enchanting location and leave with a lot of memories to treasure for a lifetime
